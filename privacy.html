Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

1. Overview

This Policy explains how Kensano (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our website and services.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data – name, email, login details.
  • Order data – products purchased, payment status, delivery info.
  • Technical data – IP, browser, device, logs, cookies.

3. How we use data

  • To provide and deliver digital products and services.
  • To process payments and send invoices.
  • To secure the platform, detect fraud, and prevent abuse.
  • To respond to support, chat, and contact requests.

4. Cookies & tracking

We use essential cookies for security and session management. Analytics cookies may be used to understand usage patterns; these never store raw payment details.

5. Third-party processors

We may share limited data with payment gateways (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal, etc.) and infrastructure providers solely to provide the service.

6. Data retention

We retain invoices and order records for legal and tax reasons. Other data is stored only as long as required for active accounts or open disputes.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at privacy@kensano.in.
