Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Select a product on the Products page or contact us through the form for custom work. We’ll confirm scope and payment.

Which payment methods do you support?

We plan to support Razorpay, Stripe, UPI and PayPal. Crypto payments may be available for selected orders.

How are digital products delivered?

Through secure download links or account access, sometimes via encrypted email/message for sensitive data.

Can you sign NDAs?

Yes, for enterprise or sensitive projects we can sign non-disclosure agreements.

Do you offer support after delivery?

Yes. Each order includes a basic support window; extended maintenance is available as an add-on.
