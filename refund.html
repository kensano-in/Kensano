Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

1. General

Kensano sells digital products and custom services. Because many items are delivered instantly or require manual work, refunds are limited.

2. Digital products (numbers, scripts, assets)

  • Once a product is delivered or downloaded, it is generally non-refundable.
  • Refunds are only considered if:
    • the product was not delivered, or
    • the product is clearly defective or not as described.

3. Custom services (editing, coding, websites, shayari)

  • For custom work, we may charge a non-refundable advance.
  • If you cancel before work has started, we may refund the remaining amount.
  • After draft delivery, refunds are not guaranteed; we focus on revisions instead.

4. Refund window

Requests must usually be raised within 7 days of purchase. After that, refunds are rarely possible.

5. How to request a refund

  1. Contact support at billing@kensano.in with your order ID.
  2. Describe the issue and attach screenshots if relevant.
  3. We review and respond within 3–5 business days.

6. Chargebacks

Unjustified chargebacks may lead to suspension of access to Kensano services.
