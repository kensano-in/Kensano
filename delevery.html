Shipping & Digital Delivery Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

1. Nature of products

All Kensano products are digital (numbers, scripts, assets, edits, websites). No physical shipping is required.

2. Delivery methods

  • Secure download link inside your Kensano account.
  • Encrypted message or email for sensitive items (e.g., numbers).
  • Access credentials for hosted websites or tools.

3. Delivery times

  • Instant digital products: usually within a few minutes after payment confirmation.
  • Custom work: timelines are stated on the order or proposal (e.g., 3–7 working days).

4. Failed delivery

If you have not received access or download instructions within the promised time, contact support@kensano.in with your order ID.
