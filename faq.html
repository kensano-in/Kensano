Operational Questions

Delivery speed, revisions, privacy, payment, and more.

How fast is delivery?

Most services are delivered within 2–24 hours, depending on complexity, queue, and revision scope. Larger website or system builds may take longer.

Which services are available?

We currently offer alternate numbers, script writing, video editing, website building, coding utilities, and shayari / creative writing. Custom hybrid projects are also possible.

Do I get revisions?

Yes — most services include at least one refinement pass. Larger projects may include structured feedback loops defined at the start.

How do payments work?

You pay through trusted, encrypted gateways such as UPI, Razorpay, Stripe, or other mutually agreed methods. No card data is stored by us directly.

Is everything confidential?

Yes. We treat every project as confidential by default. No work, data, or ideas are shared publicly unless you explicitly approve.

Do you handle illegal or spam-based work?

No. We do not support illegal, abusive, or deceptive use-cases. All services must align with legal and ethical standards.

Can I cancel or request a refund?

Refunds are handled as per our Refund Policy. In general, we support fair refunds in cases of non-delivery or clear technical failure.

Can I request a fully custom project?

Yes. Use the Contact page to outline your idea. We respond with a structured proposal and timeline.
